About Khur Travel

Your Trusted Mongolia Travel Specialist Since 2007

At Khur Travel, we're passionate about sharing the authentic beauty and culture of Mongolia with travelers from around the world. With over 15 years of experience, our team of local experts is committed to creating unforgettable journeys that connect you with the true spirit of this remarkable land.

Our Story

Khur Travel was founded in 2007 by a group of passionate Mongolian travel enthusiasts with a shared vision: to showcase the authentic beauty of Mongolia to the world while supporting local communities and preserving our precious natural environment.

What began as a small operation has grown into one of Mongolia's most respected travel companies, serving thousands of satisfied travelers from across the globe. Throughout our journey, we've maintained our commitment to quality, authenticity, and sustainability.

Our name "Khur" comes from the traditional Mongolian horsehead fiddle, an ancient string instrument that symbolizes our rich cultural heritage. Just as the khuur creates beautiful melodies that touch the soul, we strive to create travel experiences that resonate deeply with our guests.

Our Philosophy

We believe that travel should be transformative, enriching, and responsible. Every tour we design is crafted with these principles in mind:

Authenticity: We create genuine cultural connections between travelers and local communities, offering insights into Mongolia's nomadic traditions that can't be found in guidebooks.

Expertise: Our guides are passionate locals with deep knowledge of Mongolia's history, culture, and natural wonders, ensuring you receive the most informed and engaging experience possible.

Sustainability: We're committed to environmentally responsible tourism practices that respect and preserve Mongolia's pristine landscapes for future generations.

Personalization: We understand that every traveler is unique, which is why we offer both carefully curated itineraries and fully customized experiences tailored to your specific interests.
