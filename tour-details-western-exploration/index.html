Tour Overview

Experience the untamed beauty of Western Mongolia on this comprehensive 9-day adventure. From pristine alpine lakes and soaring mountains to ancient petroglyphs and rich cultural experiences, this journey takes you through the most spectacular landscapes of Mongolia's westernmost provinces. Meet diverse ethnic groups including Kazakhs, Tuvans, and Khotons, witness traditional eagle hunting, and discover the unique traditions that have been preserved for centuries in this remote corner of Asia.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore Khar Us Lake National Park, home to diverse bird species and wildlife
  • Visit authentic Kazakh and Tuvan families to experience their nomadic lifestyle
  • Witness traditional eagle hunting demonstrations with local Kazakh hunters
  • Discover ancient petroglyphs and archaeological sites in the Altai region
  • Explore Tsambagarav Mountain National Park with its snow-capped peaks
  • Experience local culture through traditional music, cuisine, and handicrafts

Itinerary

Day 1: Ulaanbaatar to Khovd

Morning flight from Ulaanbaatar to Khovd, the main city of Western Mongolia. Upon arrival, meet your guide and driver and enjoy lunch at a local restaurant. In the afternoon, visit the Khovd Museum to learn about the region's diverse ethnic groups and rich history. Dinner and overnight at a comfortable local hotel.

Day 2: Khovd to Khar Us Lake National Park

After breakfast, drive to Khar Us Lake National Park, one of Mongolia's largest freshwater lake systems and an important bird habitat. Enjoy a picnic lunch by the lakeshore and spend the afternoon exploring the lake area by boat, observing various bird species including rare relict gulls and Dalmatian pelicans. Visit a local Khoton family to experience their traditional lifestyle. Overnight in a comfortable ger camp by the lake.

Day 3: Khar Us Lake to Mankhan Sand Dunes

Continue your journey to the impressive Mankhan sand dunes, a unique ecosystem where desert meets steppe. Hike to the top of the dunes for panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes. In the afternoon, visit a local camel herding family and experience a short camel ride if desired. Learn about traditional dairy products and their preparation methods. Overnight in a ger camp near the dunes.

Day 4: Mankhan to Tsambagarav Mountain

Drive to Tsambagarav Mountain National Park, dominated by the snow-capped Tsambagarav Mountain (4,208m). The area is known for its spectacular alpine scenery and diverse wildlife. Visit a Kazakh family and learn about their unique traditions, including embroidery and felt-making. Enjoy traditional Kazakh hospitality and cuisine. Overnight in a family homestay or nearby ger camp.

Day 5: Eagle Hunting Experience

Spend a full day with Kazakh eagle hunters learning about this ancient tradition that has been preserved for centuries. Witness a demonstration of eagle hunting techniques and learn how these magnificent birds are trained from an early age. Participate in the preparation of traditional Kazakh dishes for lunch. In the evening, enjoy traditional music and throat singing performances. Overnight in the same accommodation.

Day 6: Tsambagarav to Tolbo Lake

Journey to the beautiful Tolbo Lake, a freshwater lake surrounded by mountains at an elevation of 2,080m. Along the way, visit the Khoid Tsenkher Cave featuring ancient rock paintings dating back to the Paleolithic period. Enjoy lunch in a picturesque spot and arrive at Tolbo Lake by late afternoon. Set up camp by the lakeshore and enjoy dinner under the stars.

Day 7: Tolbo Lake to Ölgii

Morning hike around Tolbo Lake before continuing to Ölgii, the capital of Bayan-Ölgii province and the cultural center of Kazakhstan in Mongolia. Visit the local market to see traditional crafts and goods. In the afternoon, explore the town and visit a local mosque, reflecting the predominantly Muslim Kazakh population. Check into a comfortable hotel in Ölgii for the night.

Day 8: Ölgii and Surrounding Area

Visit the impressive Altai Tavan Bogd National Park visitor center to learn about the region's unique flora, fauna, and geography. In the afternoon, visit a workshop where traditional Kazakh handicrafts are made, with the opportunity to purchase authentic souvenirs. Enjoy a special farewell dinner featuring traditional music performances. Overnight at your hotel in Ölgii.

Day 9: Ölgii to Ulaanbaatar

Morning flight from Ölgii back to Ulaanbaatar. Transfer to your hotel or connect with your onward journey. Tour concludes upon arrival in Ulaanbaatar.
