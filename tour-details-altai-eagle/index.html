Tour Overview

Embark on an unforgettable adventure to Western Mongolia's Altai Mountains region, where you'll experience the unique culture of Kazakh eagle hunters and the breathtaking landscapes of the Altai Mountains. This 7-day tour combines natural beauty with authentic cultural experiences.

You'll have the opportunity to stay with eagle hunter families, witness ancient hunting traditions, trek through stunning mountain scenery, visit pristine lakes, and explore remote villages. Throughout the journey, you'll gain insight into the region's diverse ethnic groups and their traditional way of life.

This tour is perfect for adventure seekers, photography enthusiasts, and cultural explorers who want to experience one of Mongolia's most spectacular and culturally rich regions. With experienced local guides, comfortable accommodations, and carefully planned activities, you'll discover the best that Western Mongolia has to offer.

Tour Highlights

  • Stay with authentic Kazakh eagle hunter families in their traditional homes
  • Witness eagle hunting demonstrations and learn about this ancient tradition
  • Trek through the dramatic landscapes of the Altai Mountains
  • Visit stunning alpine lakes including Khoton-Khurgan and Dayan
  • Experience the warm hospitality of nomadic families
  • Explore ancient petroglyphs at Shiveet Uul
  • Enjoy traditional Kazakh cuisine and cultural performances
  • Visit the vibrant provincial capital of Ulgii and its local markets

TOUR ITINERARY

Day-by-day plan for your Altai Mountains & Eagle Hunters adventure

Day 1: Ulaanbaatar to Ulgii

Flight, City Tour

Your journey begins with a morning domestic flight from Ulaanbaatar to Ulgii, the capital of Bayan-Ulgii province (3 hour flight). Upon arrival, you'll be greeted by your local guide and transferred to your hotel.

After lunch at a local restaurant, enjoy a city tour of Ulgii, including the local museum, mosque, and vibrant market where you can see traditional Kazakh handicrafts. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with traditional Kazakh cuisine and a cultural performance featuring traditional music and dombra (stringed instrument) playing.

Accommodation: Hotel in Ulgii

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Day 2: Ulgii to Eagle Hunter Family

Cultural Experience

After breakfast, drive through picturesque countryside to a Kazakh eagle hunter family (approx. 3-4 hours). Upon arrival, you'll be welcomed by the family and settled into your accommodations.

Spend the afternoon learning about the ancient tradition of eagle hunting, including how eagles are trained and the special relationship between hunters and their birds. You'll have the opportunity to witness a demonstration of eagle hunting skills and perhaps even try holding a trained golden eagle on your arm (with assistance).

In the evening, enjoy dinner with the family and learn about their way of life through conversations with your guide as translator.

Accommodation: Family Homestay or nearby Ger Camp

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
