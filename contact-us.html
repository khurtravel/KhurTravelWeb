Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Mongolia?

The peak travel season in Mongolia is from June to August when the weather is warmest. May and September offer fewer crowds and pleasant temperatures. Winter travel (November-March) is possible for experienced travelers looking for unique experiences.

Do I need a visa to visit Mongolia?

Many nationalities require a visa to enter Mongolia. Some countries have visa exemption agreements. We recommend checking with your local Mongolian embassy or consulate for the most up-to-date information based on your nationality.

How far in advance should I book my Mongolia tour?

For summer tours (June-August), we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, especially for Naadam Festival tours. For shoulder season travel, 2-3 months is usually sufficient.

Is Mongolia safe for tourists?

Mongolia is generally a safe destination for tourists with low crime rates in rural areas. As with any destination, basic precautions should be taken, especially in Ulaanbaatar where pickpocketing can occur in crowded areas.
