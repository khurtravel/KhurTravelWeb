Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Khur Travel Mongolia. These Terms and Conditions govern your relationship with Khur Travel Co., Ltd and your use of our services. By booking or participating in our tours, you agree to be bound by these terms. Please read them carefully before making a reservation.

1. Booking and Payment Terms

1.1 Reservation Process

To secure your reservation, we require the following:

  • A completed booking form with all required details for each participant.
  • A non-refundable deposit of 30% of the total tour cost at the time of booking.
  • Full payment is due 60 days prior to the tour departure date.
  • For bookings made within 60 days of departure, full payment is required at the time of booking.

1.2 Payment Methods

We accept payments via:

  • Credit/Debit Card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer

For bank transfers, all transfer fees are the responsibility of the client. Please refer to our Payment Information page for detailed instructions.

1.3 Confirmation

Your booking is confirmed only after we receive both your deposit and completed booking form. We will then send you a confirmation letter with detailed information about your tour.

2. Cancellation and Refund Policy

2.1 Client Cancellation

If you need to cancel your booking, the following cancellation fees apply:

  • More than 60 days before departure: Loss of deposit (30% of total tour cost)
  • 59-31 days before departure: 50% of total tour cost
  • 30-15 days before departure: 75% of total tour cost
  • 14 days or less before departure: 100% of total tour cost (no refund)

All cancellations must be received in writing via email to info@tourmongolia.com.

2.2 Khur Travel Cancellation

Khur Travel reserves the right to cancel any tour due to insufficient participation, force majeure events, or safety concerns. In such cases:

  • We will offer you an alternative tour of equivalent or similar standard and value.
  • If the alternative is not acceptable, we will refund all payments made to Khur Travel.
  • Khur Travel is not responsible for any incidental expenses or consequential losses that you may have incurred as a result of the booking, such as visas, vaccinations, non-refundable flights, or loss of enjoyment.

2.3 Tour Amendments

If you wish to make changes to your booking after confirmation, we will do our best to accommodate your request. However, any changes are subject to availability and may incur additional costs.

Travel Insurance Requirement

Travel insurance is mandatory for all our trips. Your insurance must provide coverage for personal injury, medical expenses, emergency repatriation, and personal liability. We also strongly recommend it covers cancellation, curtailment, and loss of luggage and personal effects.

3. Tour Itineraries and Changes

3.1 Itinerary Flexibility

While we strive to operate all tours as described, we reserve the right to change any trip itinerary, departure date, or any part of specific itineraries due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Natural disasters
  • Political instability
  • Other unforeseen circumstances that may affect the safety or enjoyment of the trip

Any changes made will be to enhance your overall experience or ensure your safety. We will notify you of significant changes before departure or as soon as possible if changes occur during the tour.

3.2 Price Changes

We reserve the right to amend our prices in the event of significant currency fluctuations, government tax increases, or other unforeseen costs increases. If the price increase is greater than 10% of the total tour cost, you may choose to:

  • Accept the new price
  • Cancel your booking and receive a full refund
  • Accept an alternative tour of comparable standard, if available

5.1 Pre-Tour Health Requirements

It is your responsibility to:
