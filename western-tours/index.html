Western Mongolia Tours

Explore Mongolia's Majestic Altai Region

Western Mongolia is home to the magnificent Altai Mountains, pristine alpine lakes, and diverse ethnic groups with unique traditions. This remote and rugged region offers breathtaking landscapes, encounters with eagle hunters of the Kazakh people, and opportunities to experience traditional cultures preserved for centuries. Our Western Mongolia tours take you off the beaten path to discover one of Asia's last true wilderness areas.
