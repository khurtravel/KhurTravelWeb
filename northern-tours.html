Northern Mongolia Tours

The "Mongolian Switzerland"

Northern Mongolia is a land of pristine wilderness, with dense taiga forests, crystal-clear lakes, and rugged mountains. Often called the "Mongolian Switzerland," this region offers breathtaking landscapes and unique cultural experiences. Explore Lake Khövsgöl, one of Asia's largest freshwater lakes, discover traditional Tsaatan reindeer herder communities, and enjoy the lush beauty of Mongolia's northern frontier.
